Turbo Race – Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 9, 2026

Turbo Race ("the game", "we", "us") is developed by Carlos Pinan. This privacy policy explains what information is collected when you play the game and how it is used.

Information We Collect

Turbo Race does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Specifically:

  • No account registration is required.
  • No name, email address, or contact information is collected.
  • No location data is accessed.
  • No device identifiers are collected by the game.

Data Stored Locally

The game saves the following data only on your device:

  • Your best score per difficulty level (Easy, Normal, Hard).
  • Sound mute preference.

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

Google Play Games Services

Turbo Race integrates Google Play Games Services for optional leaderboard functionality. If you choose to sign in, Google may collect information in accordance with their own privacy policy. We do not receive or store your Google account information.

For details, see: Google Privacy Policy

Third-Party Services

No third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking libraries are included in Turbo Race.

Children's Privacy

Turbo Race does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. Since no personal data is collected at all, the game is safe for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new "Last updated" date.
