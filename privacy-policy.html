Turbo Race – Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 15, 2026

Turbo Race ("the game", "we", "us") is developed by Carlos Pinan. This privacy policy explains what information is collected when you play the game and how it is used.

Information We Collect Directly

Turbo Race itself does not require account registration and does not directly collect your name, email address, or location. The game's own code does not assign or read persistent device identifiers.

Data Stored Locally

The game saves the following data only on your device:

  • Your best score per difficulty level (Easy, Normal, Hard).
  • Sound mute preference.

This data stays on your device and is not sent to us directly.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

Turbo Race shows ads using Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers (such as the advertising ID), IP address, and app usage data to serve ads, including personalized ads based on your interests, subject to your consent choices.

If you are in the EEA, UK, or Switzerland, you will be shown a consent form (via Google's User Messaging Platform) before any personalized ads are served, and you may opt for non-personalized ads instead.

You can review or reset your ad personalization choices at any time in your device's Google Settings, or via Google's Ad Settings.

For details, see: How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services

Google Play Games Services

Turbo Race integrates Google Play Games Services for optional leaderboard and achievement functionality. If you choose to sign in, Google collects information in accordance with their own privacy policy to provide this feature (e.g. your Play Games profile and submitted scores). We do not receive or separately store your Google account information.

For details, see: Google Privacy Policy

Third-Party Services Summary

Turbo Race uses the following third-party services, each of which may collect data as described in their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob — advertising
  • Google Play Games Services — leaderboards and achievements

No other analytics or tracking libraries are included.

Children's Privacy

Turbo Race is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because the game serves ads via Google AdMob, users identifying as under 13 (or the applicable age in their region) should not be shown personalized ads; this is handled via Google's family policies and consent settings.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new "Last updated" date.
