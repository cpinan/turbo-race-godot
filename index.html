Why play Turbo Race?

🏎️

Three difficulty modes

Easy, Normal, and Hard — each with its own obstacle density, speed curve, and leaderboard.

🏆

20 achievements

From your first jump to cumulative milestones — synced with Google Play Games Services.

📊

Global leaderboards

Compete with players worldwide. Separate leaderboards per difficulty keep competition fair.

🎮

Two control schemes

On-screen joystick for precision or accelerometer tilt for hands-free play. Switch any time.

🆓

Free to play

No pay-to-win, no energy timers. A small banner ad keeps the lights on.

📴

Works offline

Full gameplay without internet. Scores and achievements sync when you reconnect.

Simple to learn, hard to master

Jump over obstacles, dodge lane blockers, and react to air hazards that only hurt you at peak height. The world speeds up the longer you survive.

Every obstacle type has a distinct collision rule pulled directly from the original Cocos2d-x source — no guesswork, full parity.

3 obstacle types — ground, lane, air
665 scripted entries per level
100 points per avoided obstacle
20 achievements to unlock

Built with Godot 4

A full port of the original Cocos2d-x / C++ game into Godot 4.7 + GDScript. Static typing throughout, pure physics functions, 128 unit tests, and zero new mechanics — just clean parity.
